Server

Server looks for reports in directoty:

GameDir/frId/ReportsDir

places orders in: 

GameDir/fId/OrdersDir

where "fId" - is a faction name. For instance one may set:

GameDir  /home/user/game/players
ReportsDir reports
OrdersDir  orders

then for faction f013 it's reports may be found at:

/home/user/game/players/f013/reports
